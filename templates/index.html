SignAI

SignAI is an innovative AI-based application that bridges the communication gap between the hearing and the deaf communities. By using advanced machine learning and deep learning techniques, SignAI can recognize and translate sign language in real time from any device.

About SignAI

Our Mission

SignAI aims to make communication accessible for everyone. By combining advanced computer vision, deep learning architectures, and natural language processing, our AI bridges the gap between spoken and signed communication. We believe that every person deserves to be understood—regardless of language, background, or physical ability.

Our long-term vision is a world where sign language is no longer a barrier. Whether in classrooms, workplaces, hospitals, or everyday interactions, SignAI seeks to provide seamless, real-time translation that empowers individuals and strengthens human connection.

The first full-featured version is planned for January 2026, supporting approximately 20,000 words, advanced contextual understanding, and the ability to translate long and complex sentences with high accuracy. This release will be available on all major devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop systems.

Note: SignAI is still in an early development stage and can currently translate sentences of up to about 15 words. The model was trained on the RWTH-PHOENIX-Weather dataset (compressed version) and currently recognizes around 800 German Sign Language (DGS) words. At this prototype stage, the system focuses mainly on individual words and short phrases, so inaccuracies, limited vocabulary, and partial sentence translations may occur. As the dataset expands, the architecture improves, and training becomes more extensive, SignAI will gradually deliver more accurate and complete sentence translations.

Advanced Technology

Our AI is trained on tens of thousands of video samples from diverse and carefully curated datasets. Using state-of-the-art transformer models, spatio-temporal convolutional networks, LSTMs, Seq2Seq-Models, and attention mechanisms, SignAI can understand subtle hand shapes, movement transitions, and facial expressions. These components are essential features of sign languages and allow the system to capture meaning far more precisely than traditional gesture-recognition tools.

The adaptive learning model is designed to recognize regional variations, personal signing styles, and different levels of signing speed. SignAI continuously improves through research, real-world usage data, and in future from user feedback. With every update, the system becomes faster, more accurate, and more capable. If you you are more interested about the technical details, visit our Github page.

Building Bridges

Our goal goes far beyond translation. SignAI is built to support inclusive communication in education, healthcare, government services, and daily life. We want to empower individuals who rely on sign language, support interpreters, and provide hearing people with an easy way to understand and engage with the Deaf community.

By breaking communication barriers, we foster equality, accessibility, and connection. SignAI is not just a tool—it is a step toward a more inclusive world where everyone can share their voice and be heard.

Desktop

The desktop version of SignAI provides a powerful environment for testing, recording, and analyzing sign language. It allows users to record custom videos and instantly receive accurate translations.

With our desktop application, you get access to advanced features including real-time processing, detailed analytics, and the ability to save and review your translation history. The intuitive interface makes it easy for both beginners and professionals to communicate effectively.

Mobile

Our mobile version of SignAI brings the power of real-time translation directly to your pocket. Using your smartphone's camera and flash, the app delivers incredible results and enables seamless communication anywhere.

The mobile app is designed with accessibility in mind, offering a clean and simple interface that works in any lighting condition. Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, SignAI Mobile ensures you can communicate effectively without barriers.
